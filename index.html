October #Beatz

An Annual concert of MFF

The first #Beatz in 2014 was held with the purpose of creating a memorable milestones in 2-year development of Music For Friends. It represented the enthusiasm and hard works of all team members throughout the year.

#Beatz14 has received tons of love and encouragement from audience; therefore, we decided to turn it into an annual concert where people could freely showcase their talent, follow passion and develop professional skills.

“I was amazed by how creative the show was. While I fell in love with romantic part in the beginning, I was thrilled by the later part, when everyone stood up, danced and screamed with excitement, especially with the dance performance and the rock band in the end. Everyone was on fire!!!” - Thai Nguyen, an audience of October #Beatz15

New Dawn

Things are about to change when the sun raises.

Audience will experience many up and down feeling in different period of life. By remixing and smashing up, we would like to blow a new wind to the old songs, make them divergent, but still familiar.

#Beatz16 has been prepared more elaborately and professionally in order to present the best concert ever.

It is not only a performance but also our passion and vision.

OCTOBER #BEATZ is the biggest music concert of Music For Friends where audiences live the night with music and joy.
